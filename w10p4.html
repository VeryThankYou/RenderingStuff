Pros of WebGPU:
  • Free to use and sell WebGPU products
  • Highly customizable
Cons of WebGPU:
  • Poor documentation, lack of support
  • Not optimized aswell as blender
  • Difficult to add lighting and camera position, as you are working with coordinates
  • Difficult to debug
  • No recursion calls
  • Limited amount of storage buffers
Pros of Cycles in Blender:
  • Free to use and sell WebGPU products
  • Optimized for production meaning you can produce something of quality reasonable fast
  • Millions of models in different fileformats that you can import that are ready to use in Blender
  • Very easy to change camera position
  • Raytracing is built-in, so all you have to do is add objects
  • Lots of documentation and you can get support easily
Cons of Cycles in Blender:
  • Since it is an engine, not as customizable as WebGPU
